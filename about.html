History

Founded in 2015, TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a 100-person meetup has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences in the industry, now hosting over 5,000 attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical implementation. We believe in creating an environment where innovation meets application, fostering connections between industry leaders, developers, and visionaries.

Our commitment to diversity, inclusion, and technological advancement drives us to curate content that pushes boundaries while remaining accessible to all skill levels.
